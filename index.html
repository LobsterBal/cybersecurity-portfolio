JOURNEY & ASPIRATION

In this section, I will share about my learning journey and career objective.

Journey

Throughout my journey in cybersecurity, I’ve gained hands-on experience in various domains, including networking, penetration testing, and vulnerability assessment. I’ve become proficient in tools like Wireshark, Metasploit, and Shodan, which have helped me better understand network traffic, security flaws, and threat actors. My exposure to technologies such as VPNs, IPSEC, and GNS3 has enhanced my practical knowledge in configuring secure networks and simulating network setups. However, there are areas where I still face challenges, such as advanced cryptography techniques and real-time vulnerability exploitation. To improve in these areas, I plan to dedicate more time to practice using real-world environments and participate in Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges to sharpen my skills. Additionally, I plan to dive deeper into threat detection and analysis, which will help me develop a more comprehensive approach to cybersecurity.

Aspiration

My goal is to become a skilled cybersecurity professional specializing in network security, penetration testing, and vulnerability management. I aspire to work with an organization that values proactive security measures and where I can contribute to building robust security infrastructures. By continuously learning and applying the latest security practices and tools, I aim to help businesses stay ahead of emerging threats and protect sensitive data from cyber-attacks.
